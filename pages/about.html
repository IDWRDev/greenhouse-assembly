Our community

Growing people who grow in Christ.

The GreenHouse Assembly is a welcoming space for biblical formation, prayer, worship, service and genuine Christian community.

Welcome

Rooted in faith. Open in spirit.

We exist to make room for people to encounter Jesus, develop a steady life of faith and serve one another with humility. This is an introduction to the ministry’s direction, prepared for public use while formal historical and leadership details are confirmed.

Our story

A place to be planted and strengthened.

Like a greenhouse, healthy growth is nurtured with care, light, patience and community. We are building a ministry environment where people can take meaningful next steps in faith.

Mission

To cultivate Christ-centred lives through biblical teaching, prayerful worship, discipleship and practical care.

Vision

To be a spiritually healthy community where people grow, serve and carry hope into everyday life.

Core values

Values that shape our life together.

Biblical formation

Truth with grace

We want Scripture to form both our understanding and our everyday choices.

Prayer

Dependence on God

We make space to listen, intercede and bring every part of life before God.

Community

People matter

We seek relationships marked by welcome, dignity, encouragement and care.

Service

Faith in action

We look for practical ways to serve our neighbours and contribute with our gifts.

Growth

Whole-life discipleship

We encourage growth of spirit, mind and body with wisdom and compassion.

Hospitality

A broad welcome

We honour Black global identity, Nigerian heritage and every person seeking a place to belong.
