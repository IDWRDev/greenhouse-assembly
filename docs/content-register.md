## Status definitions

- **Verified**: supported directly by owner-provided material in the repository.
- **Verified public theme**: already published on the current site but should receive owner/doctrinal review before expansion.
- **Derived draft planned**: original editorial work that remains subject to owner review.
- **Media simulation planned**: non-playable replacement-ready visual/content system.
- **Pending owner review**: title or concept exists, but source material is insufficient for substantive claims.
