# Sprint 4 Phase A — Source Audit and Content Map

## Audit result

The repository contains the public website, its images and logos, accessibility/privacy/terms documents, and the following public pages: Home, About, Ministries, Teaching, Resources, Events, Give, Contact, legal pages, and 404.

No manuscripts, sermon scripts, teaching outlines, book files, media files, leadership biographies, factual event schedules, contact details, or giving-provider details were found in the repository during this audit.

The current site consistently establishes these themes:

- faith, formation, and flourishing;

- whole-person formation: spirit, mind, and body under Christ;
- truth, prayerful dependence, hospitality, care, excellence, and practical service;
- Black global identity, Nigerian heritage, and broad welcome;
- growth that leads to wise leadership, creative excellence, and public responsibility.

The following project titles are already present in the site and must be treated as **pending owner review** until source documents are supplied:

## Content architecture

All future editorial work will connect to five dimensions:

1. **Know** — Scripture, Gospel, covenant, redemption, and Christian truth.
2. **Become** — prayer, character, wisdom, discipline, and spiritual maturity.
3. **Belong** — worship, hospitality, family, fellowship, and mutual care.
4. **Serve** — ministry, leadership, creativity, outreach, and practical service.
5. **Influence** — vocation, work, family, society, culture, and public responsibility.
