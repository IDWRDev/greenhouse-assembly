# Social Preview Plan

## Rules

- Apply brand text in HTML/CSS or a controlled production file, never rely on AI-generated lettering.
- Use a 1200 × 630 WebP or JPG source with a separate accessible page title and description.
- Do not add canonical URLs until the final public domain is confirmed.
- Do not show dates, speakers, attendance, availability, or operational claims in a social image.
