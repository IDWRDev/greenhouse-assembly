## Approved production prompts

### Teaching series: The Architecture of Redemption

Use case: `historical-scene`.

Asset type: 16:9 teaching-series thumbnail, 1600 × 900 source, with room for HTML overlay text.

Primary request: A warm documentary-cinematic study scene for a theological teaching series about covenant and redemption: a Black Nigerian scholar in a contemporary library, seated at a large table with an open Bible and unmarked archival-style maps; stone, parchment, deep green, soft gold and natural wood; thoughtful, calm, intelligent, no visible written text.

Constraints: dignified contemporary African representation, natural hands, no watermark, no logos, no lettering inside the image, no fake historical claim.

### Teaching series: Spiritual Formation

Use case: `photorealistic-natural`.

Asset type: 16:9 teaching-series thumbnail, 1600 × 900 source, with room for HTML overlay text.

Primary request: A contemplative early-morning scene of a Black Nigerian woman reading an open Bible beside a softly lit window, simple home setting, warm natural light, deep green and muted-gold accents, documentary realism, quiet prayerful atmosphere, no visible written text.

Constraints: natural skin texture, realistic hands and Bible pages, no watermark, no logos, no generated lettering, no overdramatic lighting.
